About Me

From Athlete to Your Coach

I'm Umesh Upadhyay — an International Certified Health Coach & Nutritionist with a passion for transforming lives through fitness. My journey began as a competitive powerlifter, where I earned national-level medals, and evolved into becoming a Men's Physique athlete ranked in the top 5 nationally.
